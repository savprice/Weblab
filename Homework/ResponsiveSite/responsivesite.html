Why is Proper Skincare Important?

Skincare is one of the most crucial parts of your everyday routine, and here's why:

  • Skin sheds itself daily
  • All skin types are different
  • Beautiful skin is a lifelong process (minimize wrinkles and lines in the future)
  • Saves you money for the future
  • When you look good, you feel good
  • Help you establish other routines

Isn't it Expensive?

Yes, and no. The first time you splurge on new skincare products, it can and will definitely put a dent in the bank account. However, once you purchase the products, they usually last for around 2-4 months, which gives you enough time to save up for when you need to repurchase. One of the reasons skincare is important is that it helps you save money for the future, meaning that if you take care of your skin from an early age, you won't experience the same issues you would in 35 years if you hadn't spent the extra money right now. For example, if you take care of your skin now, you can prevent many wrinkles and fine lines, which many people would spend hundreds of dollars on botox for. Trust me, it's worth it.
